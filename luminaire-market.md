<p><p>The luminaire market comprises various types of lighting fixtures, each with its own unique characteristics. LED luminaires are energy-efficient and durable, making them popular for both residential and commercial use. CFL luminaires use compact fluorescent bulbs, providing a longer lifespan than incandescent bulbs. LFL luminaires use linear fluorescent tubes, commonly found in offices and commercial spaces. HID luminaires utilize high-intensity discharge lamps and are often used in outdoor and industrial settings. Halogen luminaires use halogen bulbs, known for their high-quality light output. Incandescent luminaires, although less energy-efficient, are still commonly used for their warm and familiar light.</p></p>
