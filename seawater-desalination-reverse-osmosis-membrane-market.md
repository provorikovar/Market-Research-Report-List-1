<p><p>The seawater desalination reverse osmosis membrane market consists of two types of membranes: composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are made up of multiple layers, with the top layer acting as a barrier to resist fouling and the inner layer providing high water permeability. Asymmetric membranes, on the other hand, have different pore sizes on their surfaces, allowing for better separation of salt and other impurities. Both types of membranes play crucial roles in the desalination process, removing salt and purifying seawater.</p></p>
