The Rotary Flower Basket Method involves cleaning the silicon core/silicon rod by placing it inside a rotating flower basket while using a high-pressure water flow. This method is effective in removing contaminants. On the other hand, the Pure Water Isolation Method isolates the silicon core/silicon rod in a chamber filled with pure water, which helps in effectively removing impurities without causing damage. Both methods serve the purpose of ensuring clean and high-quality silicon cores/silicon rods.</p></p>
