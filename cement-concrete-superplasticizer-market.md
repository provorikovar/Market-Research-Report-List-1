<p><p>Cement Concrete Superplasticizer is a chemical used to enhance the workability and performance of concrete by reducing water content. There are three types of superplasticizers commonly used in the market. Aliphatic water reducing agents work by adsorbing onto cement particles, reducing the surface tension and allowing for better dispersion of the particles. Naphthalene series superplasticizers are made from naphthalene sulfonate and are effective in reducing water content and increasing the slump of concrete. Polyether monomer superplasticizers have excellent dispersing properties and can improve the flow and strength of concrete.</p></p>
