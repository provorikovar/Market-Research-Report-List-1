<p><p>The flush car door handles market comprises three main types: spiral hidden door handles, translational hidden door handles, and others. Spiral hidden door handles are designed with a spiral mechanism that allows the handle to pop out when needed and retract when not in use, providing a sleek and streamlined appearance. Translational hidden door handles work similarly, but instead of a spiral mechanism, they use a linear motion to extend and retract. The "others" category includes innovative designs that do not fit into the spiral or translational types, offering unique and diverse options for car door handle solutions.</p></p>
