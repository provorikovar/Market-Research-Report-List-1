<p><ul><li>Blanking</li><li>Embossing</li><li>Bending</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive metal stamping refers to the process of shaping metal sheets into various forms to create automotive components. There are three main types of metal stamping in the automotive market: blanking, embossing, and bending. Blanking refers to cutting metal sheets into desired shapes. Embossing involves creating raised or sunken designs on the metal surface. Finally, bending is the process of deforming the metal sheet to a specific angle or shape. These techniques play a crucial role in manufacturing car body parts and other components used in automobiles.</p></p>
