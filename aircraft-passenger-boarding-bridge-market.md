<p><p>An Aircraft Passenger Boarding Bridge, also known as a jet bridge or airbridge, is a movable connector used to bridge the gap between an aircraft and a terminal building, enabling passengers to board and disembark directly from the terminal without having to go outside. It provides a safe and convenient passage for passengers, ensuring smooth and efficient operations at airports.</p><p>The future outlook of the Aircraft Passenger Boarding Bridge Market is expected to be promising.
