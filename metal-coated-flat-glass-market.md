<p><ul><li>Vacuum Magnetron Sputtering</li><li>Vacuum Evaporation Method</li><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Coated Flat Glass Market is divided into three types: Vacuum Magnetron Sputtering, Vacuum Evaporation Method, and Chemical Vapor Deposition. Vacuum Magnetron Sputtering refers to the process of coating the flat glass with metal by using magnetrons in a vacuum chamber. Vacuum Evaporation Method involves evaporating the metal in a high vacuum environment and allowing it to condense on the glass surface. Chemical Vapor Deposition utilizes chemical reactions to deposit a metal coating on the glass. These methods are widely used in the metal coating industry for flat glass applications.</p></p>
