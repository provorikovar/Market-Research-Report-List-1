<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Engine oils are categorized into single-grade and multi-grade types in the market. Single-grade oils have a consistent viscosity throughout the temperature range and are suitable for specific climate conditions. They are denoted by a single numerical viscosity rating, such as SAE 30 or SAE 40. On the other hand, multi-grade oils have a wider viscosity range and are designed to provide consistent performance in both cold and hot conditions. They are labeled with two numbers, such as SAE 10W-30, indicating their viscosity at different temperatures.</p></p>
