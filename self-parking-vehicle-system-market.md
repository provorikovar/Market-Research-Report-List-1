<p><p>The self-parking vehicle system market comprises various types of sensor systems. Ultrasonic sensor system uses sound waves to detect obstacles around the vehicle, enabling it to park safely. Radar sensor system utilizes radio waves to measure the distance between the vehicle and surrounding objects, accurately guiding the parking process. Image sensor system employs cameras to capture and process visual data, enabling the vehicle to analyze and navigate its surroundings during parking. Lastly, other sensor systems may include Lidar or infrared technology, which provide additional perception capabilities for safe and efficient self-parking.</p></p>
