<p><p>The Acoustic-Insulation Rock Wool market offers various types of materials for sound insulation. Felty rock wool is a soft and fibrous material that provides excellent sound absorption. Strip rock wool is a thinner variant, usually presented in the form of strips, suitable for narrow spaces. Tubular rock wool is designed specifically for insulation in pipes, offering excellent thermal and acoustic properties. Granular rock wool consists of small particles that can be easily poured or blown into cavities, making it a versatile choice for insulating irregularly shaped areas.</p></p>
