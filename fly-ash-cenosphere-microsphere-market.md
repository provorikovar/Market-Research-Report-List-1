<p><p>The fly ash (cenosphere) microsphere market can be categorized into types based on their structure: solid and hollow microspheres. Solid microspheres are compact particles with a continuous solid interior. On the other hand, hollow microspheres have a less dense, hollow interior surrounded by a solid shell. These different types offer distinct properties and applications. Solid microspheres provide greater strength and thermal resistance, making them suitable for applications such as resin fillers and coatings. Hollow microspheres, with their lightweight and insulating properties, are commonly used in industries like construction, oil and gas, and automotive for applications such as lightweight fillers, thermal insulation, and buoyancy aids.</p></p>
