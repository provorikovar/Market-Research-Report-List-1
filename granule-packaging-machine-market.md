<p><p>The granule packaging machine market comprises two types: semi-automatic and fully automatic machines. Semi-automatic machines require some human intervention during the packaging process, such as manual loading and unloading of granules. On the other hand, fully automatic machines are capable of performing all packaging tasks independently, including filling, sealing, and labeling. Semi-automatic machines are suitable for small-scale operations with limited production volume, while fully automatic machines are better suited for large-scale industrial applications that require high efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
