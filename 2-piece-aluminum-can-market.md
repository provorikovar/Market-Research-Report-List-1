<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>There are two main types of aluminum cans in the market. The first type is DRD (Draw and Redraw) cans, which are made by drawing a flat sheet of aluminum into a cup shape and then redrawing it to achieve the desired dimensions. These cans are commonly used for beverages and are known for their versatility and cost-effectiveness. The second type is DWI (Drawn and Wall Ironed) cans, which are made by drawing the aluminum sheet into a cup shape and then ironing the walls to achieve a thinner and stronger can. DWI cans are widely used in the packaging industry due to their high strength-to-weight ratio and ability to store carbonated beverages.</p></p>
