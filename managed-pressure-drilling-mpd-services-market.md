<p><p>Managed Pressure Drilling (MPD) Services market consists of various techniques, such as Mud Cap Drilling (MCD), Dual Gradient Drilling (DGD), Constant Bottom Hole Pressure (CBHP), Return Flow Control Drilling (RFCD), and others. Mud Cap Drilling involves injecting mud into the well, forming a column that prevents formation fluids from flowing. Dual Gradient Drilling employs multiple fluids to achieve controlled pressure. Constant Bottom Hole Pressure maintains a specific pressure while drilling. Return Flow Control Drilling allows continuous circulation of fluid, reducing formation damage. Other techniques include pressurized mud cap drilling, underbalanced drilling, among others.</p></p>
