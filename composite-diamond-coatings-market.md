<p><p>Composite diamond coatings are advanced coatings used in various industries, known for their exceptional hardness, durability, and wear resistance. Two popular market types for composite diamond coatings are Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD). CVD involves the use of chemical reactions to deposit diamond coatings onto surfaces, while PVD utilizes physical processes like evaporation or sputtering. Both techniques offer unique benefits, such as improved thermal stability, adhesion, and reduced friction, making them suitable for applications in cutting tools, automotive components, aerospace, and more.</p></p>
