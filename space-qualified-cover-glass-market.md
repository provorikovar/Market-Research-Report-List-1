CMG refers to continuous monolithic glass cover, which boasts enhanced strength and durability. Finally, CMO stands for continuous monolithic optical glass, which offers superior optical properties for applications requiring exceptional clarity and precision. These market types cater to the specific needs of space technology and contribute to advancements in satellite systems, telescopes, and other space-related equipment.</p></p>
