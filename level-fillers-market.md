<p><p>Level fillers are machines used in various industries to fill products into containers at a consistent level. There are two main market types for level fillers: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic level fillers are fully automated machines that perform the filling process without the need for human intervention. They are highly efficient and can handle large production volumes. In contrast, semi-automatic level fillers require some human assistance in terms of container placement and activation. They are suitable for smaller production quantities or when a higher level of control is desired.</p></p>
