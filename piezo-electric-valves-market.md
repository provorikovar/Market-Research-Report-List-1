<p><p>Piezo-electric valves are a type of valve that operates using a piezoelectric mechanism, which converts electrical energy into mechanical motion. In the market, there are three main types: two-way piezo-electric valves, three-way piezo-electric valves, and others. </p><p>Two-way piezo-electric valves have two ports, allowing for the control of fluid flow in either an open or closed position. Three-way piezo-electric valves, on the other hand, have three ports, enabling the regulation of flow between two different paths. Lastly, the "others" category includes piezo-electric valves with unique features or designs catering to specific applications, such as high-temperature environments or compatibility with corrosive fluids.</p></p>
