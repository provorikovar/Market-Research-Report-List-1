<p><p>Phytogenics, also known as botanicals or plant-based feed additives, refer to a group of natural substances derived from herbs, spices, and other plants that are used in animal feed to improve their performance and overall health. These additives act as natural growth promoters, enhance feed efficiency, boost immunity, and improve digestion in animals. Phytogenics are primarily used in poultry, swine, ruminants, and aquaculture feed.</p><p>The global phytogenics market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory during the forecast period. Several factors are driving this growth. Firstly, the increasing consumer preference for natural and organic products has led to a rise in demand for phytogenics as an alternative to chemical feed additives. Additionally, the ban on antibiotic growth promoters in many countries has boosted the adoption of phytogenics as a safer and healthier option for animal nutrition.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising awareness about the negative impact of antibiotic overuse on animal and human health has encouraged livestock producers to shift towards phytogenics. These additives help to reduce the need for antibiotics and can improve animal performance without compromising welfare. Moreover, the growing demand for meat and dairy products due to the increasing population and changing dietary habits in emerging economies is driving the need for efficient and sustainable animal production, thereby contributing to the market growth.</p><p>In terms of regional analysis, Europe holds a significant share in the phytogenics market, driven by strict regulations on antibiotic use in livestock production and the presence of major market players. North America and Asia Pacific are also witnessing substantial growth due to the increasing demand for natural feed additives and the growing awareness about animal welfare.</p><p>Overall, with the aforementioned drivers and the projected CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period, the phytogenics market is expected to experience continued expansion and innovation in the coming years.</p></p>
