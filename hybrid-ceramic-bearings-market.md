<p><p>Hybrid ceramic bearings market is divided into two types based on the materials used: Si3N4 material and non-Si3N4 material market. Si3N4 material refers to bearings made from silicon nitride ceramic, offering high strength and excellent thermal properties. They are used in demanding applications that require resistance to extreme temperatures and corrosion. On the other hand, the non-Si3N4 material market includes bearings made from other ceramic materials or hybrid combinations, offering different properties and advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
