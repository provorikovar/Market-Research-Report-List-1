<p><p>High Molecular Weight Polyisobutylene (HMW PIB) is a key product in the global polyisobutylene market. It is commonly classified into two types: C-PIB and HR-PIB. C-PIB, or conventional PIB, is produced by cationic polymerization and is used in various applications such as adhesives, sealants, and lubricants. HR-PIB, or hydrogenated PIB, undergoes additional refining processes to remove residual impurities, resulting in improved stability and thermal resistance. HR-PIB finds applications in the production of fuel and lubricant additives, as well as in the adhesives and sealants industry.</p></p>
