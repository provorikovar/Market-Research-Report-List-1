<p><p>Metallocene Polypropylene (mPP) is a specialized form of polypropylene that is synthesized using a metallocene catalyst. It offers enhanced mechanical properties, higher clarity, and improved processability compared to conventional polypropylene. There are two main types of metallocene polypropylene: Metallocene Isotactic polypropylene (miPP) and Metallocene Syndiotactic Polypropylene (msPP). miPP is characterized by its crystalline structure, high melting point, and excellent stiffness, making it suitable for applications such as packaging films and fibers. On the other hand, msPP has a more amorphous structure, offering improved impact resistance, flexibility, and transparency, making it ideal for automotive and medical applications.</p></p>
