<p><p>The diesel engine market can be categorized into two types: single cylinder and multi-cylinder engines. Single cylinder engines have one cylinder in which the combustion process takes place, resulting in a simpler and less complex design. They are typically used in small applications such as motorcycles and small equipment due to their compact size and lower power output. On the other hand, multi-cylinder engines have multiple cylinders that fire in a specific sequence, providing higher power and efficiency. They are commonly found in larger vehicles such as trucks and buses where greater power is required.</p></p>
