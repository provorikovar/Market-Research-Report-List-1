<p><strong>The Low Profile Solenoids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Face</li><li>Conical Face</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-profile solenoid market can be categorized into two types based on the shape of their faces. Flat face solenoids have a flat surface, creating a straight path for the plunger to move in and out. These solenoids are commonly used in applications where a large force is required. On the other hand, conical face solenoids have a tapered surface, providing a gradually increasing force as the plunger moves closer to the face. These solenoids are used when a more controlled and precise force is needed.</p></p>
