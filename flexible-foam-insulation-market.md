<p><p>The flexible foam insulation market can be divided into several types based on the material used in their production. These include NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) based insulation, EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) based insulation, chloroprene based insulation, and others. NBR based insulation is known for its excellent thermal resistance and flexibility. EPDM based insulation offers resistance against weather and UV rays. Chloroprene based insulation is ideal for applications that require resistance against chemicals and oils. Other types of flexible foam insulation may include polyester, polyether, or polystyrene based solutions.</p></p>
