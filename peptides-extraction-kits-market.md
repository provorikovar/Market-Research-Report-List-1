<p><p>The Peptides Extraction Kits market offers various types of kits based on different sorbents used during the extraction process. Three common types of sorbents used in these kits are C18, C8, and Silica. C18 sorbents are made of a carbon chain that helps retain hydrophobic peptides. C8 sorbents also retain hydrophobic peptides, but have a shorter carbon chain than C18. Silica sorbents are used for both hydrophobic and hydrophilic peptides. Additionally, there are other types of sorbents available in the market for specific extraction needs.</p></p>
