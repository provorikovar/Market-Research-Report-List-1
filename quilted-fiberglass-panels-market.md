<p><p>Quilted fiberglass panels are used for thermal and acoustic insulation in various industries. They come in different market types: one-sided and double-sided. One-sided panels have insulation material quilted on one side, providing a cost-effective solution for applications where only one surface needs to be insulated. On the other hand, double-sided panels have insulation material quilted on both sides, providing enhanced thermal and acoustic insulation. Double-sided panels are commonly used when both surfaces require insulation, offering maximum efficiency and effectiveness in insulation applications.</p></p>
