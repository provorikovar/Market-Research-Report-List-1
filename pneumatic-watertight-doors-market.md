<p><p>The pneumatic watertight doors market includes two primary types: heavy watertight doors and light watertight doors. Heavy watertight doors are designed for large-scale applications, such as those found in ships and submarines. These doors provide robust protection against water ingress and are typically operated mechanically or hydraulically. On the other hand, light watertight doors are used in smaller watercraft, where weight considerations and ease of operation are important. These doors are generally made from lightweight materials and are operated pneumatically, offering reliable water sealing capabilities.</p></p>
