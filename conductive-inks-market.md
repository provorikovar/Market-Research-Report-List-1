<p><p>Conductive inks are a type of ink that can conduct electricity. They are made by infusing conductive materials, such as silver or copper particles, into a liquid medium. One of the main factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for printed electronics in various industries. Furthermore, the increasing demand for flexible and stretchable electronics is expected to provide new growth opportunities for the market in the coming years.</p><p>In conclusion, the conductive inks market is poised for steady growth during the forecast period.
