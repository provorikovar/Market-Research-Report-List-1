<p><ul><li>Interior Digital Mirror</li><li>Outside Digital Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive digital mirror market includes two types: interior digital mirror and outside digital mirror. The interior digital mirror is a technology that replaces the traditional rearview mirror inside the vehicle with a digital display screen, providing a real-time video feed from a rear-facing camera. This allows for better visibility, especially in cases of obstructed vision. Conversely, the outside digital mirror employs cameras and displays to replace the traditional side mirrors of a vehicle, offering enhanced visibility and reducing blind spots for improved driving safety.</p></p>
