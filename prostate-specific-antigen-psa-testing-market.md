This test is widely used as a screening tool for prostate cancer.</p><p>The current outlook of the PSA testing market is positive. <p><p>PSA testing is a common diagnostic tool used to detect prostate cancer. Two main types of PSA testing methods are radioimmunoassay and enzyme immunoassay. Radioimmunoassay measures the levels of PSA in a patient's blood by using radioactive markers that bind to the antigen. Enzyme immunoassay, on the other hand, uses enzymes to detect the presence of PSA in a patient's blood. Both methods provide valuable information about prostate health and help in the early detection of prostate cancer.</p></p>
