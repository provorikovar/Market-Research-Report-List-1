<p><p>BOPP Cross Bottom Bags market includes two types: Valve Cross Bottom Bags and Open Cross Bottom Bags. Valve Cross Bottom Bags are equipped with a valve for convenient filling and emptying of products. They are commonly used for packaging granular materials like rice or animal feed. On the other hand, Open Cross Bottom Bags do not have a valve and are typically used for packing powdery substances such as flour or cement. Both types of bags are made from BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film, known for its durability and resistance to moisture.</p></p>
