<p><p>Machinable ceramic market types can be categorized into three main groups: fluorophlogopite glass ceramic, non-oxide ceramic, and others. Fluorophlogopite glass ceramic refers to a type of ceramic material that contains fluorophlogopite crystal structures, which enhance its machinability and mechanical strength. Non-oxide ceramic, on the other hand, includes ceramics made from non-metallic compounds such as carbides, nitrides, and borides. This category offers improved thermal conductivity and hardness compared to traditional oxide-based ceramics. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses various types of machinable ceramics that may not fit into the specific fluorophlogopite or non-oxide classifications.</p></p>
