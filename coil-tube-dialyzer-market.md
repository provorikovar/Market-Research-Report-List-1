<p><p>A coil tube dialyzer is a type of dialyzer that is used in hemodialysis procedures for the treatment of kidney failure. The dialyzer helps in removing waste products, excess fluids, and toxins from the blood, replicating the natural function of the kidneys.</p><p>The coil tube dialyzer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
