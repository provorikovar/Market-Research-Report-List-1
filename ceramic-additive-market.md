<p><p>The Ceramic Additive market can be divided into several types, including Dispersant, Lubricant, Grinding Aid, and Others. Dispersants are additives used to evenly distribute ceramic particles in a liquid medium, ensuring uniformity in the final product. Lubricants reduce friction during the shaping or processing of ceramics, enhancing the overall efficiency. Grinding aids improve the grinding process by increasing the efficiency and reducing energy consumption. Finally, the "Others" category includes additives with various functions, such as binders, defoamers, and preservatives, which cater to specific ceramic manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
