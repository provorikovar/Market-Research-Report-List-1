<p><p>The automotive blower motor market consists of two main types of motors: brushed motors and brushless motors. A brushed motor utilizes brushes to transfer electrical currents, creating the magnetic field that drives the motor. On the other hand, a brushless motor uses electronic sensors and a controller to provide the necessary power without the need for brushes. While brushed motors are simpler and cheaper, brushless motors offer improved efficiency, reliability, and lifespan. Both types play a crucial role in powering automotive heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems.</p></p>
