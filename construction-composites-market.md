<p><p>The construction composites market is categorized based on two key types - fiber type and resin type. Fiber type refers to the material used to reinforce the composites, which can include glass, carbon, natural, or other fibers. Resin type, on the other hand, refers to the matrix material that binds the fibers together, such as polyester, vinyl ester, epoxy, or other resins. These two types play a crucial role in determining the properties and performance of construction composites in various applications.</p></p>
