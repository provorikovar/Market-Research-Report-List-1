<p><p>High-boom excavators are heavy construction equipment used for digging and excavating tasks. There are two types of high-boom excavator markets: crawler and wheeled. Crawler excavators are equipped with tracks, allowing them to move smoothly on challenging terrains like mud or slopes. They offer stability and often have larger digging capacity. On the other hand, wheeled excavators have tires, making them more maneuverable and suitable for working on paved surfaces. They are ideal for shorter distance travel and working in urban areas where flexibility and speed are crucial. Both types cater to different construction needs.</p></p>
