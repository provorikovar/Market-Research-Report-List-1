<p><p>The premium sports watch market is divided into two main types: digital watches and quartz watches. Digital watches utilize electronic displays to show time, date, and other functions. They often include features such as alarms, timers, and stopwatches. On the other hand, quartz watches rely on a quartz crystal oscillator to track time accurately. They usually have a traditional analog dial but can also include additional digital features. Both digital and quartz watches cater to consumers who value durability, accuracy, and advanced functionalities in their sports watches.</p></p>
