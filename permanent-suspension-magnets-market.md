<p><p>Permanent suspension magnets are widely used in industries such as mining, recycling, and manufacturing to remove ferrous materials from conveyor belts and chutes. There are two main types of suspension magnets available: manual cleaning and self-cleaning. </p><p>Manual cleaning magnets require operators to manually remove the collected ferrous materials by using a handle or a lever. On the other hand, self-cleaning magnets feature a self-cleaning belt or an overband, which automatically removes the ferrous materials from the magnet surface, improving efficiency and reducing downtime. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific industry requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
