<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The technical insulation market can be categorized into three types: flexible foam, rigid foam, and man-made mineral fiber. Flexible foam insulation is often used to insulate pipes, air ducts, and walls, providing thermal and acoustic insulation. Rigid foam insulation is commonly used in construction applications, such as insulating walls, roofs, and floors. It offers excellent thermal resistance and durability. Man-made mineral fiber insulation, made from materials like fiberglass and rock wool, is used in industrial and commercial settings to provide fire resistance and thermal insulation.</p></p>
