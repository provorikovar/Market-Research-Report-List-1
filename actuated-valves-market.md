<p><p>The actuated valves market can be categorized into different types based on the type of actuators used. One such type is pneumatic actuators, which use compressed air or gas to operate the valve. Electric actuators, on the other hand, use electricity to drive the valve's opening and closing mechanism. Apart from these, there are other types of actuators available in the market, such as hydraulic actuators and manual actuators, which operate the valve using hydraulic pressure or manual force, respectively. These different types provide options for users to choose the most suitable actuated valve based on their specific application requirements.</p></p>
