<p><p>The veterinary laser equipment market consists of three types: Class 2, Class 3, and Class 4. Class 2 lasers are considered safe for both patients and operators, emitting low power for superficial treatments. Class 3 lasers provide a greater range of treatment options with their moderate power and can be used for both surface and penetrating treatments. Class 4 lasers offer high power and are used for deep tissue treatments, though they require caution as they can potentially cause harm to the eyes and skin if not handled properly.</p></p>
