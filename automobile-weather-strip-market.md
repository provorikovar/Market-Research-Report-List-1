<p><strong>The Automobile Weather Strip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPDM Strip</li><li>TPE/TPO/TPV Strip</li><li>Other Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile weather strip market consists of three main types: EPDM strip, TPE/TPO/TPV strip, and other strip. EPDM strip is made of Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer rubber, offering excellent weather resistance and durability. TPE/TPO/TPV strip refers to Thermoplastic Elastomer/Thermoplastic Olefin/Thermoplastic Vulcanizate strip, known for its flexibility and recyclability while providing good sealing properties. Lastly, the other strip category includes various materials like PVC, silicone, and neoprene, among others. These strips are used to seal gaps in car doors, windows, and hoods, ensuring insulation, noise reduction, and protection against external elements.</p></p>
