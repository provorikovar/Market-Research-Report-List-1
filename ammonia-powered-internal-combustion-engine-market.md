<p><p>The ammonia-powered internal combustion engine market comprises three main types: spark-ignition engines, compression-ignition engines, and gas-turbine engines. Spark-ignition engines ignite a mixture of ammonia and air using a spark plug, while compression-ignition engines combust ammonia and air through compression, without a spark. Gas-turbine engines utilize ammonia as the fuel source in a combustion process to generate mechanical power. These different engine types offer varying benefits and applications in the ammonia-powered engine market, catering to the diverse needs of industries and sectors.</p></p>
