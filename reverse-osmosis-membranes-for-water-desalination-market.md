<p><p>Reverse osmosis membranes are used in the desalination of water to remove salt and impurities. There are two main types of reverse osmosis membranes in the market: composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are made from multiple layers, with a thin and dense selective layer that acts as a barrier to separate water from salts. Asymmetric membranes, on the other hand, have a gradient structure with varying pore sizes, allowing for high water permeability while still efficiently removing contaminants. Both types play important roles in the water desalination process.</p></p>
