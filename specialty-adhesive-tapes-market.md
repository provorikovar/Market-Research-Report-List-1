<p><strong>The Specialty Adhesive Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Hot-Melt based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty Adhesive Tapes market types can be classified into three categories: solvent-based, hot-melt based, and water-based. Solvent-based tapes are made by dissolving adhesives in solvents, providing strong bonding properties. Hot-melt based tapes are made of thermoplastic adhesives that melt when heated and solidify upon cooling, offering quick adhesion. Water-based tapes use water as a carrier for the adhesive, making them environmentally friendly and safer to use. All three types are widely used in various industries for their specific advantages and applications.</p></p>
