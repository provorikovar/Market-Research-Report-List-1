<p><p>The Rail Transit Train Control System market consists of four main types: Train Control System, Interlocking System, Dispatch Centralized System, and Centralized Monitoring System. The Train Control System ensures the safe operation of trains by regulating speed and maintaining a safe distance between them. The Interlocking System prevents conflicting movements of trains by controlling switches and signals. The Dispatch Centralized System monitors and controls train movement on a wider scale. Lastly, the Centralized Monitoring System collects and analyzes data for improved overall operation and maintenance of the rail transit system.</p></p>
