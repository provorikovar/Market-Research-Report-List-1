<p><p>The automotive composite market encompasses two main types: thermoset and thermoplastics. Thermoset composites are composed of materials that undergo curing or hardening through a chemical reaction. These composites offer high dimensional stability, good corrosion resistance, and excellent strength. On the other hand, thermoplastics are composites made of materials that can be melted and re-molded multiple times without any alteration in their properties. They provide flexibility, impact resistance, and easier processing. Both thermoset and thermoplastics composites play crucial roles in automotive applications, contributing to lightweighting, improved fuel efficiency, and enhanced structural performance.</p></p>
