<p><p>The heavy-duty damper market consists of two main types: hydraulic and pneumatic. Hydraulic dampers use the resistance of fluids to control the movement of heavy machinery and equipment, providing smooth and controlled motion. On the other hand, pneumatic dampers utilize compressed air or gas to dampen and control the movement of heavy loads. Both types of dampers are crucial in various industries such as automotive, construction, and aerospace, ensuring safety, stability, and efficient operation of heavy-duty machinery and equipment.</p></p>
