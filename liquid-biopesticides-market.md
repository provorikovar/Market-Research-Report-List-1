<p><p>Liquid biopesticides are organic solutions derived from biological sources such as bacteria, fungi, viruses, and plant extracts, which are used to control and eliminate pests and diseases in agriculture. The rising concerns over food safety and sustainability are driving the adoption of biopesticides in agriculture.
