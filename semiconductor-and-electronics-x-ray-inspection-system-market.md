<p><p>Semiconductor and Electronics X-Ray Inspection Systems are used to detect defects, cracks, and other imperfections in electronic components and semiconductors. There are two types of X-ray Inspection systems: 2D X-ray Inspection and 3D X-ray Inspection. </p><p>In 2D X-ray Inspection, a two-dimensional image of the object under inspection is generated, providing a top-down view that helps identify surface defects. </p><p>On the other hand, 3D X-ray Inspection creates a three-dimensional visual representation, allowing for a more comprehensive analysis of the internal structures and hidden defects within the object. This enables more accurate inspection and identification of complex issues such as voids, soldering problems, and component misalignments.</p></p>
