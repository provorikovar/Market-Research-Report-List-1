<p><p>The Tablet Functional Coatings market is categorized into four types, namely Cellulose Polymer, Vinyl Derivatives, Acrylic Polymer, and Other Polymers. Cellulose Polymer coatings are derived from cellulose, offering good film-forming properties. Vinyl Derivative coatings are made from vinyl compounds and provide excellent adhesion and durability. Acrylic Polymer coatings are derived from acrylic derivatives, offering high gloss and good scratch resistance. Other Polymers refer to coatings made from various other polymer materials that possess specific properties suitable for tablet applications. Each type of coating offers unique advantages and is used to enhance the functionality and performance of tablets.</p></p>
