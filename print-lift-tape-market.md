<p><p>Print lift tape is a type of adhesive tape used in the printing industry to lift printed materials without causing damage. There are two main types of print lift tape: transparent tape and frosted tape. Transparent tape is see-through and ideal for use on glossy or shiny surfaces. It provides a clear view of the printed material. On the other hand, frosted tape has a textured surface that diffuses light and reduces glare. It is commonly used on matte or textured printed surfaces to avoid reflections and improve visibility. Both types serve the purpose of lifting printed materials effectively.</p></p>
