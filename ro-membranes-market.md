<p><p>The RO membranes market consists of different types of membranes used in reverse osmosis processes. Composite membranes are a combination of different materials and provide a balance between cost, durability, and performance. These membranes are widely used in various industries like water treatment, desalination, and food and beverage processing.</p></p>
