<p><p>The Tubing Head Spools market encompasses two main types: the Model of Single Completion and the Model of Dual Completion. In the Model of Single Completion, the tubing head spool is designed to accommodate only one tubing string, allowing for the flow of fluids or gases from the well. Conversely, in the Model of Dual Completion, the tubing head spool is equipped to support two tubing strings, enabling the simultaneous extraction of hydrocarbons from separate zones in the well. Both types serve as vital components in oil and gas well systems.</p></p>
