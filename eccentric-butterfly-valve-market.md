<p><strong>The Eccentric Butterfly Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Eccentric</li><li>Double Eccentric</li><li>Triple Eccentric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eccentric butterfly valve market consists of three main types: single eccentric, double eccentric, and triple eccentric valves. Single eccentric valves have a shaft that is offset from the center of the valve disc, allowing for a tight seal. Double eccentric valves have two offsets, reducing friction and wear. Triple eccentric valves have a shaft with three offsets, enabling a bubble-tight seal and minimizing leakage. These different types cater to varying requirements, with higher eccentricity offering better performance but at a higher cost.</p></p>
