<p><p>A visible doorphone, also known as a video doorphone or video intercom system, is a device used for two-way communication between a visitor at the entrance of a building and the occupants inside. The display unit allows the occupants to see and identify the visitor before granting access.</p><p>The visible doorphone market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Factors driving this growth include the increasing need for enhanced security and convenience in residential and commercial buildings. Visible doorphones help in preventing unauthorized access and provide a means to communicate with visitors without physically opening the door. <p><p>The Visible Doorphone market consists of two types: wired and wireless. Wired doorphones are connected through physical cables, allowing for a reliable and uninterrupted communication between the doorphone and indoor receiver. They are generally considered more secure but require complex installation. On the other hand, wireless doorphones use radio frequency or Wi-Fi signals to transmit audio and video data, providing convenience and flexibility in terms of installation. While wireless doorphones may be susceptible to signal interference, they offer the advantage of easy relocation and are often preferred in retrofit installations.</p></p>
