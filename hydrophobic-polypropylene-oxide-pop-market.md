<p><p>Hydrophobic Polypropylene Oxide (POP) is a type of material that repels water. It can be classified into two market types: Mechanical Mixing Type and Chemical Graft Type. The Mechanical Mixing Type involves the physical blending of POP with a hydrophobic agent, resulting in enhanced water repellency. On the other hand, the Chemical Graft Type involves the introduction of hydrophobic monomers onto the POP backbone, creating a permanently hydrophobic material. Both types of POP serve various applications where water resistance is required.</p></p>
