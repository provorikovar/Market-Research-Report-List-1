<p><p>The commercial vehicle air conditioner market consists of two main types: reciprocating and rotary. Reciprocating type air conditioners use a piston and cylinder mechanism to compress and expand the refrigerant, providing cooling for the vehicle's interior. On the other hand, rotary type air conditioners employ a rotary compressor to compress the refrigerant. Both types aim to cool the commercial vehicle's cabin, but differ in their mechanism of operation.</p></p>
