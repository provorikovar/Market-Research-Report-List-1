<p><p>LED display guitar tuners come in two main market types - clip-on tuners and guitar tuner pedals. Clip-on tuners are small devices that can be easily attached to the headstock of the guitar, allowing the musician to tune the strings quickly. They usually have a built-in LED display that shows the pitch of each string. On the other hand, guitar tuner pedals are larger units that are placed on the floor during live performances. They feature LED displays and foot-switches, allowing musicians to tune their guitars hands-free while playing.</p></p>
