<p><p>Glazed porcelain tile is a type of ceramic tile that is made by firing clay at high temperatures and then applying a glaze to the surface. Additionally, the adoption of glazed porcelain tiles in sectors such as hospitality, healthcare, and retail has further boosted market demand.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the glazed porcelain tile market is the growing popularity of wood-look porcelain tiles. These tiles mimic the look and texture of natural wood, offering the warmth and beauty of hardwood floors without the maintenance and durability issues. This trend has gained traction due to the increasing preference for natural-looking materials in interior design.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the rise of large format glazed porcelain tiles.
