<p><p>The coal handling machine market consists of two main types: material handling machines and crushing machines. Material handling machines are designed to transport and handle coal throughout various stages of the coal mining process. These machines are used to load, unload, stack, and reclaim coal. On the other hand, crushing machines are specifically designed to break down larger coal pieces into smaller, more manageable sizes. Both types of machines play a crucial role in the coal handling process, ensuring efficient and safe coal transportation and processing.</p></p>
