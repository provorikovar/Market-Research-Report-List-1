<p><p>Cardiac MRI Testing Systems market is categorized into two types: Open MRI System and Closed MRI System. Open MRI System refers to a machine where the patient lies on a table and slides into a cylindrical tube that is open on both ends. This design ensures a more comfortable experience for the patient. In contrast, Closed MRI System features a machine with a narrow tunnel where the patient lies still on a table throughout the test. This type provides high-quality images but may cause discomfort for claustrophobic patients.</p></p>
