<p><p>Surveillance (CCTV) lens market types include fixed lenses, varifocal lenses, and zoom lenses. Fixed lenses have a fixed focal length, offering a fixed view angle and focus, making them suitable for monitoring specific areas. Varifocal lenses allow users to manually adjust the focal length, view angle, and focus, providing more flexibility to monitor different areas. Zoom lenses offer adjustable focal lengths, enabling users to change the view angle remotely without physically adjusting the lens, making them ideal for areas requiring close-up monitoring. Each lens type caters to various surveillance needs.</p></p>
