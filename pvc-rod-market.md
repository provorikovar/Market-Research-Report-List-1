Secondly, the expanding manufacturing sector, especially in emerging economies, is boosting the demand for PVC rods for various manufacturing processes. Additionally, the growing use of PVC rods in electrical applications, such as cable insulation and conduit piping, further contributes to market growth.</p><p>In terms of the latest trends, there is a growing focus on developing PVC rods with enhanced properties, such as higher impact resistance, improved flame retardancy, and increased heat resistance. Moreover, the increasing adoption of PVC recyclability initiatives and the development of eco-friendly PVC rod variants are also emerging trends in the market.</p><p>Overall, the PVC rod market is expected to experience substantial growth in the coming years, fueled by the increasing demand from various industries and the development of advanced PVC rod variants.
