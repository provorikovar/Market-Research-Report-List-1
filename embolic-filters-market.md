<p><strong>The Embolic Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Upper Chamber Embolic Filter</li><li>Inferior Chamber Embolic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embolic Filters market consists of two main types: Upper Chamber Embolic Filters and Inferior Chamber Embolic Filters. Upper Chamber Embolic Filters are designed to be placed in the upper chamber of the heart to prevent blood clots from traveling to the brain. They provide protection against strokes and other embolic events. On the other hand, Inferior Chamber Embolic Filters are placed in the lower chamber of the heart to prevent blood clots from reaching the lungs. They are primarily used to avoid pulmonary embolism. Both types of filters are important in preventing potentially life-threatening complications related to blood clots.</p></p>
