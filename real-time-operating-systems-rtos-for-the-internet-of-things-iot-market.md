<p><p>Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS) for the Internet of Things (IoT) enable efficient control and management of connected devices and data processing in real-time. In terms of market types, there are three categories:</p><p>1. Hard Real-Time Operating System: This type guarantees strict timing constraints, ensuring critical tasks are completed within specific time limits.</p><p>2. Firm Real-Time Operating System: It allows some flexibility in meeting timing requirements, allowing certain non-critical tasks to occasionally miss deadlines.</p><p>3. Soft Real-Time Operating System: This type focuses on optimizing overall system performance without strict timing constraints, as occasional missed deadlines do not affect the system's functionality.</p></p>
