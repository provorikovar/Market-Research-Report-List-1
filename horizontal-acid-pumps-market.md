<p><p>Horizontal acid pumps refer to pumps that are designed to handle acid or corrosive liquids in a horizontal direction. The market for these pumps can be divided into two types: multi-stage pumps and single-stage pumps. Multi-stage pumps have multiple impellers stacked together, allowing them to handle higher pressures and deliver liquid at a higher rate. On the other hand, single-stage pumps have a single impeller and are suitable for lower pressure and flow rate applications. Both types of pumps are used in various industries where acid handling is required.</p></p>
