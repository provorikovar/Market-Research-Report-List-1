<p><p>UT equipment, also known as ultrasonic testing equipment, is used for non-destructive testing of materials and structures by using high-frequency sound waves. UT equipment consists of various components including a transducer, pulser-receiver, and display unit, among others.</p><p>The current outlook of the UT equipment market is positive and is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
