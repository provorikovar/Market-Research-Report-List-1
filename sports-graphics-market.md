<p><p>The sports graphics market encompasses various types such as digital printing, screen printing, embroidery, and others. Digital printing utilizes computerized technology to directly transfer designs onto sports garments, providing high resolution and vibrant prints. Screen printing involves pushing ink through a mesh screen to create designs, offering versatility in terms of color and pattern choices. Embroidery involves stitching designs onto sports apparel, providing a more refined and long-lasting finish. The "others" market category includes techniques like sublimation printing, heat transfer, and vinyl printing, which offer different advantages like durability, flexibility, and customization options.</p></p>
