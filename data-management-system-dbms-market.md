<p><p>A Data Management System (DBMS) is a software application that facilitates the organization, storage, and retrieval of data. The market is divided into two types: Relational and Non-Relational. The Relational market focuses on structured data stored in tables with predefined relationships, offering SQL-based querying and strong consistency. In contrast, the Non-Relational market handles unstructured data, providing flexible schemas, horizontal scalability, and distributed systems. Both markets serve different data management needs, catering to structured and unstructured data respectively.</p></p>
