<p><p>The halogen-free flame retardant resin market includes various types such as phosphorus-based, aluminum trihydrate based, magnesium hydroxide based, and others. Phosphorus-based flame retardant resins use phosphorus compounds to inhibit the combustion process. Aluminum trihydrate-based resins release water vapor upon heating, reducing the temperature and diluting flammable gases. Magnesium hydroxide-based resins work similarly, releasing water vapor and absorbing heat. Other types, not falling under these categories, may include silicone-based or intumescent flame retardant resins, among others. These different types offer alternative options for manufacturers seeking halogen-free flame retardant solutions.</p></p>
