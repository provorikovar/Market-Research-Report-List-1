<p><p>The regenerated lead market refers to the industry that reprocesses lead scrap or waste materials to produce usable lead. Pyrometallurgy involves melting the lead scrap at high temperatures to separate impurities. Solid Phase Electrolytic Reduction utilizes an electrolytic cell to recover high-purity lead from lead oxide. Wet Smelting involves dissolving the lead scrap in a molten salt bath to separate lead and impurities. These techniques enable the recycling and reuse of lead, reducing the need for mining and the environmental impact associated with lead production.</p></p>
