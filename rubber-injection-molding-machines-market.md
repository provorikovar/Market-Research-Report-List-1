<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Rubber injection molding machines are categorized into two types: vertical type and horizontal type. Vertical type machines have a vertically oriented mold where the rubber material is injected through a vertical nozzle into the cavity. These machines are suitable for small and intricate rubber products. On the other hand, horizontal type machines have a horizontally oriented mold where rubber material is injected through a horizontal nozzle. These machines are generally used for larger rubber products and offer better accessibility for mold changes and maintenance due to their design.</p></p>
