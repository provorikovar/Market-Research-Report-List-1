<p><p>Baby Diaper Adhesives market can be classified into two main types: SBC (Styrene-Butadiene Copolymer) Based Adhesive and APAO (Amorphous Poly-Alpha Olefin) Based Adhesive. SBC-based adhesives are commonly used in the manufacturing of baby diapers as they offer high elasticity and excellent bonding strength. On the other hand, APAO-based adhesives are known for their superior resistance to heat and aging, making them ideal for applications where the diapers might be exposed to extreme temperatures. Both types of adhesives play a crucial role in ensuring the durability and functionality of baby diapers in the marketplace.</p></p>
