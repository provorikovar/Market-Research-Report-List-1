<p><p>High-intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) treatment is a non-invasive medical procedure that uses ultrasound waves to heat and destroy targeted tissues in the body. There are two main types of HIFU treatment: Ultrasound-Guided and MR-Guided. Ultrasound-Guided HIFU involves using real-time ultrasound imaging to visualize the targeted tissue and accurately deliver the ultrasound waves. On the other hand, MR-Guided HIFU uses magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) to precisely guide the ultrasound waves and monitor the treatment's progress in real-time, resulting in enhanced precision and efficacy.</p></p>
