Center positive (+) and center negative (-) connect types are two common market variations. The center positive connect type has the positive voltage on the center pin of the adapter's output connector, while the center negative connect type has the negative voltage on the center pin. The choice of connect type depends on the specific electronic device's requirements, ensuring proper compatibility and efficient power conversion.</p></p>
