<p><strong>The Automotive Magnet Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Enamelled Wire</li><li>Covered Conductor Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive magnet wire market comprises two main types of wire: enamelled wire and covered conductor wire. Enamelled wire is a type of wire that is coated with a thin layer of insulation material, such as enamel, to prevent electrical current leakage. This wire is commonly used in vehicle components like motors and transformers. On the other hand, covered conductor wire is a type of wire that has a layer of protective casing, such as nylon, to improve durability and resistance to external factors. This wire is often used in automotive wiring harnesses for enhanced performance and safety.</p></p>
