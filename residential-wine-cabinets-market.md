<p><p>The residential wine cabinets market consists of three main types: freestanding, integrated under-counter, and built-in (slot in) wine cabinets. Freestanding wine cabinets are standalone units that can be placed anywhere in the home. Integrated under-counter wine cabinets are designed to fit beneath a counter or bar, blending seamlessly into the kitchen or living area. Built-in wine cabinets are installed directly into cabinetry, creating a sleek and space-saving solution. There is also a separate market for walk-in cellars, which are larger storage spaces that can accommodate a significant wine collection.</p></p>
