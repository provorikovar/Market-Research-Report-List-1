<p><p>Pyrosequencing is a DNA sequencing method that enables accurate determination of the nucleotide sequence. It utilizes a series of enzymatic reactions to detect the incorporation of nucleotides in DNA synthesis. The principle behind pyrosequencing involves the emission of light as a result of nucleotide incorporation, which is then captured and analyzed to determine the DNA sequence. This technique has gained significant popularity in fields such as genomics, molecular biology, medical research, and personalized medicine.</p><p>The future outlook of the pyrosequencing market is promising.
