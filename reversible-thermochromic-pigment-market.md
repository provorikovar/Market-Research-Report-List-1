<p><p>The reversible thermochromic pigment market is divided into two types: organic and inorganic. Organic thermochromic pigments are made from carbon-based molecules and offer a wide range of color options. They are widely used in the food and beverage industry, textiles, and cosmetics. In contrast, inorganic thermochromic pigments are composed of metal oxides and provide stable coloring properties. They are often used in temperature sensors, security inks, and coatings. Both types of pigments change color with temperature variation, but the choice of organic or inorganic depends on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
