<p><p>Durability enhancing admixtures are additives used in construction materials to improve their resistance to weathering, chemical attacks, and other detrimental factors. Air entraining admixtures create microscopic air bubbles in concrete, enhancing its workability, freeze-thaw resistance, and durability. Corrosion inhibiting admixtures protect steel reinforcement in concrete from corrosion caused by moisture and chemicals. Shrinkage reducing admixtures minimize drying shrinkage and cracking in concrete by reducing water content. Other types of durability enhancing admixtures include water repellent admixtures and alkali-silica reaction inhibitors, which provide additional protection against moisture and alkali-silica reactions respectively.</p></p>
