<p><p>The mountain bike suspension market includes two main types: front suspension and rear suspension. Front suspension, also known as "hardtail," refers to bikes that have suspension only in the front fork. This type is suitable for riders who mainly ride on smoother terrains and want a lighter and more efficient bike. Rear suspension, also known as "full suspension," includes suspension in both the front fork and the rear frame. It is more suitable for riders who frequently encounter rough terrains, as it provides a smoother and more comfortable ride by absorbing shocks from both wheels.</p></p>
