LFO, known as lithium iron oxide, is widely used in lithium-ion batteries due to its good electrochemical properties. LNO, also called lithium nickel oxide, exhibits excellent cycling stability and energy storage capacity, making it suitable for battery applications. The "others" category encompasses other lithium supplement options available in the market, which may include lithium manganese oxide (LMO) and lithium cobalt oxide (LCO), among others. All these types serve different purposes and cater to the diverse needs of consumers in the lithium supplement market.</p></p>
