<p><p>Bio-wax is a type of wax derived from sustainable and renewable sources, such as plants, animals, and microorganisms. In the packaging industry, bio-wax is used as a coating material for food packaging, providing advantages such as improved moisture resistance and enhanced shelf life of the packaged products. In the cosmetics industry, bio-waxes are used in lip care products, lotions, and creams as natural substitutes for petroleum-based waxes, offering superior moisturizing properties.
