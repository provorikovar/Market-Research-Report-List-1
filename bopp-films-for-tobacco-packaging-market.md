<p><p>BOPP films for tobacco packaging are available in different types. Non-shrink films are used when no shrinkage is desired, maintaining the original dimensions of the packaging. Medium-shrink films provide moderate elasticity for easier packaging application. High-shrink films have high elasticity, allowing them to conform closely to the tobacco product. Printable shrink films offer the added advantage of being printable, enabling brands to incorporate attractive designs and logos on the packaging while maintaining shrink properties. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of tobacco manufacturers for packaging purposes.</p></p>
