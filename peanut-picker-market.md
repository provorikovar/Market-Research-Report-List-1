<p><p>The peanut picker market consists of two main types: self-propelled peanut pickers and automatic peanut picking machines. Self-propelled peanut pickers are operated manually by farmers to pick peanuts from the fields. They are equipped with a hopper and a conveyor belt to collect and transport the peanuts. On the other hand, automatic peanut picking machines operate with minimal human intervention. They use advanced technology such as sensors and mechanical arms to identify and pick ripe peanuts. These machines are more efficient and can save labor costs for farmers.</p></p>
