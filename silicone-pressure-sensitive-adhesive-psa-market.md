<p><p>Silicone Pressure Sensitive Adhesive (PSA) is used in various applications where a strong, yet flexible adhesive is required. Addition-cure and Peroxide-cure are two types of PSA used in the market. Addition-cure PSA provides high bond strength, excellent temperature resistance, and good adhesion properties. It is commonly used in electronic devices, aerospace, and automotive industries. On the other hand, Peroxide-cure PSA offers excellent adhesion to low surface energy substrates and is suitable for applications in the medical industry, where biocompatibility is crucial. Both types of PSAs are selected based on specific requirements and industry applications.</p></p>
