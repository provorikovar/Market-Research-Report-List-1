<p><p>The fabricated structural metal market can be categorized into two types: light sectional steel and heavy sectional steel. Light sectional steel refers to the production and distribution of fabricated metal products that are relatively lightweight and used in various applications such as roofing, framing, and decking. On the other hand, heavy sectional steel involves the manufacturing and supply of fabricated metal products that are thicker and heavier, primarily used for building construction, infrastructure projects, and industrial applications. Both types serve different purposes based on their strength and weight requirements.</p></p>
