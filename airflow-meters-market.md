<p><p>Airflow meters are devices used to measure the rate at which air flows through a system. There are two main types of airflow meters - analog type and digital type. Analog type meters provide a continuous visual representation of airflow using a needle and a scale. They are simple and cost-effective but offer limited accuracy. On the other hand, digital type meters use electronic sensors to measure and display airflow digitally. They provide more precise measurements and often offer additional features like data logging and communication capabilities.</p></p>
