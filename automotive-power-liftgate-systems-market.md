<p><strong>The Automotive Power Liftgate Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spindle Unit</li><li>ECU(Control Unit)</li><li>Touch Sensor(Pinch Sensor)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Power Liftgate Systems market consists of three prominent types: Spindle Unit, ECU (Control Unit), and Touch Sensor (Pinch Sensor). The Spindle Unit is responsible for converting rotational motion into linear motion, controlling the opening and closing operations of the liftgate. The ECU (Control Unit) coordinates and manages the functioning of various components of the liftgate system. Lastly, the Touch Sensor (Pinch Sensor) enables the liftgate to detect obstructions during operation, ensuring safety by preventing accidents or injuries. These three types play crucial roles in the efficient and safe operation of automotive power liftgate systems.</p></p>
