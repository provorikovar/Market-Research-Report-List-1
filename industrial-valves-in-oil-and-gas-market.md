<p><p>Industrial valves in the oil and gas market are crucial for controlling the flow of fluids in various processes. Ball valves use a spherical closure to regulate flow, while plug valves utilize a cylindrical or tapered plug. Gate valves have a gate-like disc that moves perpendicular to the flow path. Globe valves control flow with a movable disk in relation to a stationary ring. Butterfly valves feature a disc that pivots on a shaft to control flow. These valves play a significant role in managing fluid flow and ensuring safety and efficiency in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
