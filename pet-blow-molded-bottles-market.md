<p><p>PET blow molded bottles are categorized into two market types based on their recyclability. Recyclable bottles are made from PET material that can be collected, processed, and reprocessed into new bottles or other PET products. These bottles are environmentally friendly as they reduce waste and conserve resources. On the other hand, non-recyclable bottles are made from PET mixed with other materials, making it difficult to separate and recycle. These bottles contribute to waste accumulation and have a negative impact on the environment.</p></p>
