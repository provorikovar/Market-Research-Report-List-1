These robots are categorized into various types based on their design and functionality. </p><p>The slider type single axis robot features a sliding mechanism that allows the motor and load to move along the axis smoothly. </p><p>The slide type single axis robot incorporates sliding mechanisms on both ends of the motor, enabling it to move in both directions along the axis.</p><p>The rod type single axis robot employs a rod-shaped structure to connect the motor and load, ensuring precise and sturdy movement along the axis.</p><p>Lastly, the "Other" market segment includes single axis robots with unique designs and mechanisms that do not fit into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
