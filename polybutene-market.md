<p><p>Polybutene (PB) is a type of polymer used in various industries. It can be classified into two main market types, PB-1 and PIB (Polyisobutene). PB-1 is a copolymer of isobutylene and butene-1, known for its excellent flexibility and strong adhesive properties. It finds applications in pressure-sensitive adhesives, sealants, and lubricants. On the other hand, PIB is a homopolymer of isobutylene, offering exceptional impermeability to gases and moisture. It is commonly used in the production of fuel additives, lubricants, and adhesives. Both PB-1 and PIB are versatile polymers that cater to different industrial demands.</p></p>
