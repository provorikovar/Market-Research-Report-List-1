<p><p>Plant extracts market can be categorized into two types: non-standardized extracts and standardized extracts. Non-standardized extracts are derived from plants but do not have a fixed and consistent composition of active compounds. They may vary in potency, quality, and efficacy. On the other hand, standardized extracts are prepared using a specific and consistent formula, ensuring a predetermined concentration of active compounds. They undergo rigorous quality control measures to guarantee uniformity and reliability of the final product. Standardized extracts are preferred in industries such as pharmaceuticals and cosmetics due to their consistent potency and predictable effects.</p></p>
