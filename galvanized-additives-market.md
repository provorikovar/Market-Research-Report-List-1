<p><p>Galvanized additives are chemical compounds used in the galvanization process to enhance the performance and characteristics of galvanized coatings. The automotive industry, in particular, is creating a substantial demand for galvanized additives, as galvanized coatings are essential for corrosion protection of automotive parts.</p><p>Moreover, growing environmental concerns and stringent regulations on the use of hazardous substances are driving the demand for eco-friendly galvanized additives. Manufacturers are focusing on developing additives that are free from heavy metals and other harmful chemicals, which is expected to boost market growth.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a growing preference for water-based galvanized additives over solvent-based additives due to their low VOC emissions and environmental sustainability.
