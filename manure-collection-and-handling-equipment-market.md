<p><p>Manure collection and handling equipment market is categorized into two types: manure conveyance and manure separation/recycling. Manure conveyance equipment includes pumps, hoses, and pipelines that transport manure from livestock facilities to storage or application sites. This equipment ensures efficient and convenient movement of manure. On the other hand, manure separation/recycling equipment involves machines like separators and screens that separate solids from liquid manure. This allows for the recycling and reuse of the separated components, such as nutrient-rich solids for fertilization and liquid for irrigation or flushing systems.</p></p>
