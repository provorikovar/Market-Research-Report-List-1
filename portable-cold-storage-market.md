<p><p>The portable cold storage market consists of three main types: walk-in coolers, refrigerated containers, and refrigerated trailers. Walk-in coolers are large, stationary units typically found in restaurants and grocery stores that allow for easy access and storage of perishable items. Refrigerated containers are portable units that can be transported via trucks, ships, or trains, providing a flexible solution for transporting temperature-sensitive goods globally. Refrigerated trailers are mobile units attached to trucks, offering temporary storage during transportation and allowing for easy loading and unloading of goods.</p></p>
