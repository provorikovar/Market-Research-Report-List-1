<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive double-clutch transmission market consists of two main types: wet multiplate clutches and dry single-plate clutches. Wet multiplate clutches use oil to reduce friction and heat, ensuring smoother shifting and increased durability. On the other hand, dry single-plate clutches do not use oil and rely solely on friction for engagement, requiring regular adjustments and maintenance. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, with wet multiplate clutches offering better performance and reliability, while dry single-plate clutches are simpler and more economical.</p></p>
