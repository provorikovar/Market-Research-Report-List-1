<p><strong>The IT/Data Center Cooling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cooling</li><li>Water Cooling</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IT/data center cooling market consists of various types of cooling methods. Air cooling utilizes fans and air circulation to dissipate heat from the equipment, often through raised flooring or overhead vents. Water cooling uses water as a coolant and transfers heat away from the servers through a network of pipes or liquid-cooled plates. Other cooling methods include immersion cooling, where servers are directly submerged in a non-conductive fluid, and hybrid cooling, which combines different cooling techniques to optimize efficiency and performance. These different methods cater to the diverse cooling requirements of data centers.</p></p>
