<p><p>The heavy truck engine brake market comprises two major types, namely the Compression Release Brake and the Exhaust Brake. A Compression Release Brake, also known as a Jake Brake, operates by releasing the pressure in the cylinders during the exhaust stroke, creating a mechanical resistance that slows down the truck. On the other hand, an Exhaust Brake restricts the flow of exhaust gases, causing back pressure in the engine, which assists in deceleration. Both brakes provide additional stopping power for heavy trucks, enhancing safety and reducing wear on the traditional braking system.</p></p>
