<p><p>The Ultra Long Distance Optical Transmission Subsystem Equipment market can be categorized into two types: No SBS Suppression and SBS Suppression. No SBS Suppression refers to the equipment that does not incorporate any measures to suppress Stimulated Brillouin Scattering (SBS). SBS Suppression, on the other hand, includes the equipment that is specifically designed to minimize or eliminate the effects of SBS. SBS is an optical phenomenon that can limit the transmission distance in optical communication systems, and suppressing it can improve the overall performance and transmission distance of such systems.</p></p>
