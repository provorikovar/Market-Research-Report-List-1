<p><p>The Rubber Based Flexible Foam Market includes various types of foams such as NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) based, EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) based, chloroprene based, and others. NBR based foams are known for their excellent resistance to oil and fuel, making them suitable for applications in the automotive industry. EPDM based foams are widely used in sealing applications due to their weather resistance and good thermal stability. Chloroprene based foams offer a combination of flame retardancy and chemical resistance, suitable for use in construction and electrical industries. Other rubber-based flexible foams may include materials like silicone-based foams or natural rubber-based foams.</p></p>
