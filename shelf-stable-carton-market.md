<p><p>A shelf stable carton is a type of packaging material that is designed to store and preserve food and beverages for an extended period of time without the need for refrigeration. The market is driven by factors such as changing consumer lifestyles, urbanization, and the growing preference for packaged and processed food items. The popularity of shelf stable cartons can also be attributed to their lightweight nature, easy transportation, and recyclability.</p><p>In addition, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the growth of the shelf stable carton market.
