<p><p>The Watertight Doors and Windows market consists of two main types: Hinged Watertight Doors and Windows, and Sliding Watertight Doors and Windows. Hinged Watertight Doors and Windows are designed to swing open and closed on hinges, providing a secure seal against water intrusion. They are commonly used in marine and offshore applications. On the other hand, Sliding Watertight Doors and Windows operate by sliding horizontally, creating a watertight barrier when closed. They are often utilized in areas with limited space or where vertical movement is not feasible. Both types offer effective protection against water ingress in their respective applications.</p></p>
