<p><strong>The Microarray Scanner Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescence Microarray Scanners</li><li>Charge-coupled Devices Microarray Scanners</li><li>Colorimetric Microarray Scanners</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microarray scanner systems market comprises various types, including fluorescence microarray scanners, charge-coupled devices (CCD) microarray scanners, colorimetric microarray scanners, and others. Fluorescence microarray scanners use laser beams to excite fluorescent dyes on the slide, enabling the detection of gene expression. CCD microarray scanners capture the signal from the slide using a CCD chip, offering high-resolution scanning. Colorimetric microarray scanners capture the color changes on the slide and convert them into numerical data. Other types of microarray scanners include those based on chemiluminescence, electrochemical, and radioisotopic detection methods.</p></p>
