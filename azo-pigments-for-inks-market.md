<p><strong>The Azo Pigments for Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monoazo Pigments</li><li>Disazo Pigments</li><li>Azo Metal Complex Pigments</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Azo pigments are widely used in the ink industry due to their high color strength, excellent lightfastness, and chemical stability. These pigments are classified into different types based on their chemical structure and properties. Monoazo pigments contain a single azo group and offer bright and transparent colors. Disazo pigments have two azo groups and provide a wide range of colors, often with improved opacity. Azo metal complex pigments are created by combining azo pigments with metals, resulting in enhanced durability and heat resistance. Other types of azo pigments include modified azo pigments and condensed azo pigments.</p></p>
