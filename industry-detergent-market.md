<p><p>The detergent market can be classified into various types, such as surfactants, acids, bases, strong oxidants, enzymes, and others. Surfactants are the most common type used in detergents as they help in reducing surface tension and improving cleaning efficiency. Acids are effective in removing mineral or rust stains. Bases, on the other hand, are useful for neutralizing acidic stains. Strong oxidants like bleach help in removing tough stains and disinfecting. Enzymes, derived from natural sources, are used to break down organic matter. Other types may include additives or specialty detergents designed for specific purposes.</p></p>
