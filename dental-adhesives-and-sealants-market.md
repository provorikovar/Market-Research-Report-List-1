<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dental Adhesives and Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-etching Adhesive</li><li>Selective-etching Adhesive</li><li>Total-etching Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental adhesives and sealants are used in dentistry to provide a strong bond between dental restorations and the natural tooth structure. There are three types of dental adhesives in the market. Self-etching adhesive is a one-step process where it simultaneously removes a thin layer of the tooth surface and bonds the restoration. Selective-etching adhesive removes only the specific area that needs bonding. Total-etching adhesive is a two-step process where it removes the entire enamel layer before bonding the restoration. These adhesives cater to different requirements for varying dental procedures.</p></p>
