There are two main types of SPF on the market: Open Cell Spray Polyurethane Foam and Closed Cell Spray Polyurethane Foam. Open Cell SPF is a lightweight, porous material that provides excellent sound absorption and insulation. It is typically used in interior applications. Closed Cell SPF, on the other hand, is a denser, rigid foam that offers superior moisture resistance and thermal insulation. It is commonly used in exterior applications.</p></p>
