<p><p>An Automotive Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) is a device used in diesel engines to reduce emissions of harmful pollutants such as carbon monoxide (CO), hydrocarbons (HC), and particulate matter (PM). The DOC contains a catalyst made of precious metals such as platinum, palladium, and rhodium, which facilitate these reactions.</p><p>The market for Automotive Diesel Oxidation Catalysts is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period. By catalyst type, the market can be categorized into platinum catalyst, palladium catalyst, and rhodium catalyst. By vehicle type, the market is divided into passenger cars, light commercial vehicles, and heavy commercial vehicles.
