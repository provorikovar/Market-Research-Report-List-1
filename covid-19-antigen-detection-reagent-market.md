<p><p>The COVID-19 antigen detection reagent market encompasses two main types of testing methods: the colloidal gold method and the fluorescence immunochromatography method. The colloidal gold method involves using gold nanoparticles coupled with specific COVID-19 antibodies to detect viral antigens. This method provides rapid results and is commonly used in point-of-care testing. On the other hand, the fluorescence immunochromatography method utilizes fluorescent-labeled antibodies to identify viral antigens, allowing for easy visualization under a specialized device. Both methods are widely used in the market to detect and diagnose COVID-19 infections.</p></p>
