<p><p>Mechanical depalletizers are machines used to unload products from pallets in industrial settings. There are two main types of mechanical depalletizers: conventional depalletizers and robotic depalletizers. </p><p>Conventional depalletizers use mechanical arms or conveyors to lift and remove products from pallets. They are typically more affordable and easier to operate but may have limited flexibility and slower processing times. </p><p>Robotic depalletizers, on the other hand, use advanced robotics technology to perform the depalletizing process. They offer higher speed and versatility but can be more expensive and require skilled operators. These depalletizers are increasingly popular due to their efficiency and ability to handle a wide range of products.</p></p>
