<p><strong>The Anhydrous Borax Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Burning Borax</li><li>Fusing Hydrated Borax</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for Anhydrous Borax can be divided into two types: Burning Borax and Fusing Hydrated Borax. Burning Borax refers to the process of heating hydrated borax crystals to remove the water content, resulting in an anhydrous form. This type of borax is commonly used in welding, glass manufacturing, and ceramics due to its high melting point and low thermal expansion properties. Fusing Hydrated Borax, on the other hand, involves using hydrated borax as a flux in soldering, brazing, and welding, where it helps to remove oxides from metal surfaces and enhance the flow of molten metals. Both types have significant applications in different industries.</p></p>
