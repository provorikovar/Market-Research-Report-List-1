<p><p>The medical ready-to-use syringe market consists of two main types: glass syringes and polymer syringes. Glass syringes are made from high-quality glass materials that ensure durability, chemical resistance, and smooth operation. They are commonly used for drugs that need to be stored for a longer duration. Polymer syringes, on the other hand, are made from various polymer materials such as polypropylene or cyclic olefin copolymer. They are lightweight, break-resistant, and suitable for drugs that are sensitive to glass or require low extractables and leachables.</p></p>
