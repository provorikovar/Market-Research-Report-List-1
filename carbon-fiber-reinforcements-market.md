<p><p>Carbon fiber reinforcements are materials used to strengthen and reinforce various products. There are two main types of carbon fiber reinforcements: regular-tow and large-tow. Regular-tow carbon fiber reinforcements consist of small individual carbon filaments bound together to form a single tow. This type is commonly used in the aerospace and sporting goods industries. On the other hand, large-tow carbon fiber reinforcements consist of larger bundles of carbon filaments, making them more suitable for applications requiring high strength and stiffness, such as automotive and wind energy industries.</p></p>
