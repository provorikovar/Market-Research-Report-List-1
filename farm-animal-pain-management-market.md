These may include ointments, sprays, or topical analgesics, which provide relief from pain and inflammation. On the other hand, the internal use market involves the administration of pain management medications through oral or injectable routes. These medications work to alleviate pain by targeting the animal's nervous system or reducing inflammation internally.
