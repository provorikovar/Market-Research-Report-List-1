<p><p>Refill pumps market types can be classified into three categories: manual, semi-automated, and automated. Manual refill pumps require the user to manually operate the pump to refill a liquid substance. Semi-automated pumps have additional features like timers or volume settings but still require some level of manual intervention. Automated refill pumps are fully automated, often equipped with sensors and advanced controls that can perform the refilling process without any human intervention. These different types of pumps cater to varying needs and preferences in terms of convenience and efficiency in liquid refilling processes.</p></p>
