<p><p>N-Methyldiethanolamine (N-MDEA) is an organic compound with the chemical formula C5H13NO2. It is a clear, colorless liquid that is a tertiary amine with two hydroxyethyl groups. The market is driven by the increasing demand for gas sweetening agents in the oil and gas industry. N-MDEA is widely used as a gas sweetener to remove impurities, particularly hydrogen sulfide (H2S) and carbon dioxide (CO2), from natural gas.</p><p>Moreover, the growing demand for corrosion inhibitors in the automotive industry is also contributing to the market growth. N-MDEA is an effective corrosion inhibitor, widely used in engine coolants, metalworking fluids, and lubricants.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising demand for personal care products, such as shampoos, conditioners, and soaps, is expected to drive the market growth.
