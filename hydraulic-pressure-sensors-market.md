<p><p>Hydraulic pressure sensors are used to measure the pressure in hydraulic systems. Differential type sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points, such as the inlet and outlet of a hydraulic pump, providing information on flow rate or direction. Relative type sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, allowing for accurate measurement of absolute pressure or vacuum levels. Both types are integral in various industries, such as automotive and manufacturing, to ensure proper functioning, safety, and performance of hydraulic systems.</p></p>
