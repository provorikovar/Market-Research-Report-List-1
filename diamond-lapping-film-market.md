<p><p>Diamond lapping film is available in different types of backing, including PSA backing and plain backing. PSA backing stands for Pressure Sensitive Adhesive backing, where the film has an adhesive layer on one side, allowing it to be easily attached to a backing plate during lapping or polishing. Plain backing refers to films without any adhesive layer, requiring additional means to attach them to a backing plate. Additionally, there are other specialized markets for diamond lapping film, such as thin-film magnetic head production or fiber optic connector polishing, which have specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
