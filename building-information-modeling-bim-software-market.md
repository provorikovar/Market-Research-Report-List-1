<p><p>Building Information Modeling (BIM) software market encompasses various types. 3D BIM focuses on design modeling, allowing architects and engineers to create a three-dimensional digital representation of a building. 4D BIM incorporates construction dynamics, enabling contractors to visualize and manage the construction process, considering time and sequencing. 5D BIM introduces cost estimation capabilities, helping project managers to track and control expenses. 6D BIM encompasses built facilities, aiding facility managers in maintaining and operating the building more efficiently. Lastly, 7D BIM addresses environmental protection, integrating sustainability factors into the design, construction, and maintenance processes.</p></p>
