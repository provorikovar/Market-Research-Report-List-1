<p><strong>The Compact Benchtop Centrifuge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ventilated Centrifuge</li><li>Refrigerated Centrifuge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compact benchtop centrifuge market consists of two main types: ventilated centrifuges and refrigerated centrifuges. Ventilated centrifuges are designed to operate at room temperature, utilizing air circulation to prevent sample overheating. These centrifuges are commonly used for general laboratory applications that do not require low temperature control. On the other hand, refrigerated centrifuges are equipped with a cooling system that allows the samples to be kept at low temperatures during centrifugation. These centrifuges are essential for applications that demand temperature-sensitive sample processing, such as DNA/RNA extraction or protein purification.</p></p>
