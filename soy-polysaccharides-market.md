<p><p>Soy polysaccharides, also known as soybean polysaccharides or soy gums, are complex carbohydrate molecules derived from soybeans. They enhance the texture, consistency, and shelf life of these products. In the pharmaceutical industry, soy polysaccharides are utilized in drug delivery systems due to their biocompatibility and controlled release properties. They also find application in cosmetics as stabilizers and moisturizers.</p><p>The market analysis of the soy polysaccharides market indicates a steady growth trajectory.
