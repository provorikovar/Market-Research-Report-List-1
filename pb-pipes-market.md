<p><strong>The PB Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PB Pipes market is divided into two types: PB-H and PB-R. PB-H refers to Polybutene-1 Homopolymer, which is a type of polymer made from purely butene monomers. It is known for its high rigidity and strength, making it suitable for use in applications that require high pressure and temperature resistance. On the other hand, PB-R stands for Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer, which is a polymer made from a mix of butene and other monomers. PB-R offers improved flexibility and impact resistance, making it suitable for applications that require more durability and flexibility.</p></p>
