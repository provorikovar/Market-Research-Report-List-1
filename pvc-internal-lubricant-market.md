<p><p>PVC internal lubricants are additives used to enhance the processability and performance of PVC materials. There are different types of internal lubricants available in the market. Stearic acid, a common choice, is a fatty acid that reduces friction and improves melt flow during PVC processing. N-Butyl Stearate, another popular option, is an ester that provides excellent lubricating properties. There are also other types of internal lubricants, including metallic soaps, glycerol esters, and phosphate esters, which offer different benefits and functionalities in PVC applications.</p></p>
