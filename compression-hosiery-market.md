There are three main types of compression hosiery available in the market. Graduated compression stockings are designed to gradually increase pressure from the ankle upward. Anti-embolism stockings are used to prevent blood clots during periods of immobility. Nonmedical support hosiery, on the other hand, offers mild compression for individuals without specific medical conditions. These stockings aid in reducing leg swelling, relieving pain, and preventing varicose veins.</p></p>
