<p><p>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) Devices are used for patients with heart failure to help improve their heart's pumping efficiency. There are two types of CRT devices: Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemakers (CRT-P) and Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillators (CRT-D). CRT-P pacemakers help coordinate the heart's pumping while CRT-D defibrillators not only synchronize the heart but also provide a shock if a life-threatening arrhythmia occurs. These devices are essential in managing heart failure and ensuring proper heart function for better patient outcomes.</p></p>
