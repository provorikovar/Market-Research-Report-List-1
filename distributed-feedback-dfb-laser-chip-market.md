<p><p>The Distributed Feedback (DFB) Laser Chip Market includes two major types: Free Space Laser Diode and Fiber-Coupled Laser Diode. Free Space Laser Diodes are commonly used for applications where the laser beam travels through open space. They provide high output power and can be easily integrated into various systems. On the other hand, Fiber-Coupled Laser Diodes are designed for applications where the laser beam is transmitted through an optical fiber. They offer enhanced stability, accuracy, and efficiency, making them ideal for telecommunications, data communications, and medical industries.</p></p>
