<p><strong>The NBR Compounding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HNBR</li><li>XNBR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) compounding market has two main types: HNBR (Hydrogenated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) and XNBR (Carboxylated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber). HNBR is derived from NBR by adding hydrogen, resulting in improved chemical resistance and higher temperature capabilities. It is often used in automotive and industrial applications. XNBR, on the other hand, includes carboxyl groups, which enhance oil and abrasion resistance. It is commonly used in hoses, seals, and gaskets. Both HNBR and XNBR offer enhanced performance properties compared to traditional NBR, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
