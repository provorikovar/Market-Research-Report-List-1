<p><p>The Blood Glucose Device (SMBG) market encompasses various types of devices used for measuring blood glucose levels. Strips are a crucial component, as they are inserted into a glucose meter to provide a blood sample for analysis. The glucose meter is the main device used in SMBG, as it measures the glucose level and displays the results. Lancets are used to puncture the skin and extract a small blood sample for testing. All three components play a vital role in helping individuals with diabetes monitor their blood glucose levels accurately.</p></p>
